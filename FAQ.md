## 4. Support and Resolution Protocol

### 4.1 Where do I raise my concern initially?

Please follow this process:

- Send an email to **internship-support@vicharanashala.zohodesk.in**.
- Our support team will respond **within 2 hours** of your submission between **9:00 AM and 9:00 PM**.
- Any question submitted after **9:00 PM** will be answered on the **next day**.

### 4.2 What if I don’t receive a response within 24 hours?

If you do not receive a response within **24 hours**, you may raise a **second ticket**.

In the subject line, mention:

> **"Attention Please – Follow-up on Ticket #[Original Ticket Number]"**

This ticket will be escalated to our **Mentors Team**, who will reach out to you for a **voice call discussion** and try to resolve the issue directly.

### 4.3 Who will respond to my second ticket?

The **Mentors Team** will handle the second ticket and schedule a **voice call** to ensure your issue is clearly understood and resolved.

### 4.4 If my concern still isn’t addressed, how do I escalate further?

After **48 hours** of your first ticket (and after raising a follow-up ticket), you can escalate the matter via email to the **Internship Sponsor**, with CC to **Prof. Sudarshan Iyengar**, and the following recipients in CC:

- Your department’s **faculty reference**
- Your department **HOD**
- The **DLED Team** at **dled@iitrpr.ac.in**

Ensure that your email includes:

- Your **full name** and **department**
- **Reference numbers** and **dates** of the two Zoho Desk tickets
- A **brief explanation** of the unresolved issue

### 4.5 Why do I need to include my faculty reference and HOD in the final escalation email?

This ensures **transparency** and allows your academic mentors to support you while helping us maintain **accountability** and **timely resolution**.

### 4.6 Is this process mandatory for all issues?

Yes. To maintain consistency and ensure efficient support, we request all interns to follow this **three-step resolution path**.

## 8. Termination & Rejoining

### 8.1 I received a termination email due to overlapping academic commitments, but my exams are over now. Can I rejoin the current internship batch?

No. The internship is a **full-time commitment**, and any **academic overlap**, even if temporary, disqualifies a participant from continuing.

You will need to **apply again** for the **next available internship batch** if interested.

### 8.2 Can I appeal my termination if I have valid reasons for missing assignments or sessions?

The internship policy allows **no leniency**.

If you have already received an official termination notice due to **lack of attendance**, **late submissions**, or **academic overlap**, your internship **cannot be reinstated**.

These rules are strict to maintain **fairness** and **program quality**.

### 8.3 I plan to rejoin the internship later. Will it be the same program?

If you wish to participate again, you must **reapply** and go through the **selection process** for the next batch.

Please note that **future internships may not be funded** by any organization or **IIT Ropar**.

## 9. ViBe

### 9.1 How do I log in to the ViBe platform?

Follow these steps:

1. Use the **invite link** sent to your registered email. You will receive an email from **Vicharanashala**.
2. Open the email and click the **"Accept Invite"** button.
3. You will be redirected to the **ViBe** page.
4. Click on **"Continue as Student"**. Always log in as a **Student**.
5. After clicking "Continue as Student", you will be redirected to the next page.
6. Click on **"Continue with Google"** and sign in using your **registered email ID** (the one where you received the invite link).
7. You will then be redirected to the **Dashboard**.

---

### 9.2 I accepted the invite, but it shows “No course enrolled.” What should I do?

1. Go to the **Profile** section of your dashboard.
2. Make sure you are signed in with the **same email ID** you used during registration. You can verify this by logging into the Google account where you originally received the registration link.
3. If your institute uses an **alias email**, try logging in with the **registered ID directly**.
4. If the issue persists, inform **Support**, so they can **manually add you**.

---

### 9.3 Videos are not playing / stuck on repeat.

1. Make sure you are watching the videos in **sequential order** without skipping.
2. Allow **camera** and **microphone** access (mandatory for progress tracking).
3. Clear your browser **cache and cookies**, then refresh with **Ctrl + Shift + R**.

---

### 9.4 I am getting an “Access Restricted” error.

This usually happens if a **step is skipped**.

1. Please watch each video **in order**.
2. If the issue persists, try refreshing the page by pressing **Ctrl + R**. You may be redirected to the **dashboard**, but you can return to your course and continue from where you left off.

---

### 9.5 My quiz answers are correct, but the system is marking them wrong.

1. Note the **video number** and **case study name**.
2. Report the issue to the **Support Desk**.
3. In some cases, the issue is resolved from the **backend within a few hours**.

---

### 9.6 My dashboard shows less than 100% even after completing all videos/quizzes.

1. Sometimes, progress may take **time to update**.
2. Refresh your browser and check again.
3. In case of discrepancies, the **Google Sheet records** maintained by organizers are considered **final**.

---

### 9.7 I cannot find my course on the dashboard.

2. If you already accepted, check under **"Invites"** (top-right of the dashboard).
3. Accept the course invite by clicking the **"Invites"** button at the top-right corner of the page.
4. If you’re still unable to view your courses on the dashboard, please reach out to the **Support Desk** for assistance.

### 9.12 ViBe is not detecting my face. What should I do?

To ensure your face is properly detected by the software, please follow these guidelines:

1. Sit in a **well-lit room**.
2. Keep your **face uncovered** at all times.
3. Ensure the **background is clear** and not blurred.
4. Position the camera so that it captures your **entire face**.
5. Avoid lighting that causes **glare or shadow** on your face.
6. Confirm that your **camera is switched on** and has the necessary **permissions**.
7. Sit in a **quiet place** to avoid disturbances.
8. Ensure **no one else** is visible in the camera frame.

---

### 9.13 Why can’t I log in or access ViBe?

1. Ensure that you have **accepted the invite link** sent to your registered email ID.
2. If it still doesn’t work, refresh the page by pressing **Ctrl + R** or clear the cache by pressing **Ctrl + Shift + R**.
3. If the issue persists, contact our **Support Desk** via email or message, providing:
	- Your **name**
	- Your **registered email ID**
	- A **screenshot or screen recording** of the problem

---

### 9.14 Why do I see the “Quiz Failed” option every time I submit my answers?

This issue can occur for several reasons. You can try the following steps:

1. Refresh the page using **Ctrl + R**.
2. Log out and **log in again**.
3. If you still face the same issue, contact the **Support Desk** via email or message, providing your **name**, **registered email ID**, and a **screenshot or screen recording** of the problem.

---

### 9.15 Why do my videos auto-restart?

Videos may auto-restart due to the following reasons:

1. **Face not detected**.
2. **Blurred background**.
3. **Background noise** being detected.
4. Face is **not clearly visible**.
5. **More than one person** is detected in the camera frame.
